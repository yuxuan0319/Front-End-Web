Japanese Chicken Curry Recipe

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • Tips

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned.
  2. Add the chopped onions, carrots, and potatoes. Sauté until the onions are translucent.
  3. Add the minced garlic and ginger, and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Stir in the curry powder, coating the vegetables and chicken evenly.
  5. Pour in the chicken broth, soy sauce, and honey. Stir well to combine.
  6. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for 20-30 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Serve the curry over steamed rice.

Tips for Perfect Japanese Chicken Curry

For a thicker curry, mash some of the potatoes into the sauce.

Use chicken thighs for a juicier and more flavorful curry.

Add apple or grated pear for a subtle sweetness in your curry.
