Sushi Recipe

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • Tips

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/3 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Seaweed sheets (nori)
  • Fresh fish (salmon, tuna, etc.)
  • Cucumber, avocado, and other vegetables
  • Soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger for serving

Instructions

  1. Rinse the sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Cook the rice with 3 cups of water in a rice cooker or on the stove.
  3. Mix the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small saucepan over medium heat until the sugar dissolves. Let it cool.
  4. Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to a large bowl and gently fold in the vinegar mixture. Let the rice cool to room temperature.
  5. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat, shiny side down.
  6. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving a small border at the top edge.
  7. Add slices of fish and vegetables across the center of the rice.
  8. Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat, pressing gently to seal the edges.
  9. Cut the sushi roll into bite-sized pieces using a sharp knife.
  10. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

Tips for Perfect Sushi

Ensure your knife is very sharp to get clean cuts. Wet the knife with a bit of water between cuts to prevent sticking.

Don't overfill the sushi roll, or it will be difficult to roll and cut.

Experiment with different fillings to create your favorite sushi combinations.
